# Sample Data

Sample data will be added to this folder after the SharePoint lists are built and finalised.

---

## Data Policy

All sample data in this project is **synthetic**. No real employee names, company data, email addresses, or business records are used at any point. The sample data is designed to reflect realistic operational patterns in a luxury retail environment — including sale event names, department structures, approval workflows, and issue categories — without referencing any actual organisation or individual.

## Sample Data Design Notes

- Sale names, dates, and discount percentages are fictional but designed to be realistic for a luxury fashion retailer
- Employee names, emails, and departments follow a consistent fictional company: `luxemaison.com`
- All dates use 2026 as the reference year to maintain temporal consistency with the project timeline
- Issue categories reflect real operational support types: Access — Employee, Workflow Error, App Bug, Notification, Access — Admin
- RequestStatus values across records are distributed to support Power BI metric testing: mix of Approved, Denied, Pending, Revoked, and Pending Revocation
